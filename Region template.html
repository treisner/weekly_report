Report Notes:
  • There are individual worksheets for the region and each district in the region.
  • If there are any new writers in your region they will appear in a separate tab 'New This Week'.
    • A prescriber is considered new if the current data week is the first time in 104 weeks (2 years) that there is at least 1 Afrezza Rx.
    • If the presciber is not in the call plan, the address used comes from Symphony.
  • The writers in a region or district are selected as all writers with 1 or more scripts over the last 12 months.
  • The writers in a territory are ranked based on the last 52 weeks of Afrezza trx.
  • Share is 52 weeks of Afrezza TRx divided by 52 weeks of Rapid Only TRx.
  • The Trend Columns (R and S) have the following values based on the current 13 week TRx average compared to the previous 39 week average.
    • Growing: the current average is greater than the previous average by at least 3%
    • Declining: the current average is less than the previous average by at least 3%
    • Flat: the current average is within plus or minus 3% of the previous average
    • Gone: the current average is 0
  • Rapid Only refers to the following products
    • Afrezza
    • Apidra
    • Admelog
    • Fiasp
    • Novolog
    • Humalog
  • No prescription data or rank is shown for prescribers with an PDRP Indicator of 'Y'. These prescribers will be in the list if their rank is in the top 10 for a territory or they have more than one Afrezza script per week average, but no rank or TRx data will be shown.
  • The column "13 Week Calls" shows the number of calls recorded in the thirteen week period ending on the data data.
